Vomit

Exit by squeezing the contents of the stomach of living things through the mouth and sometimes through the nose. These contents can be eaten food or other substances in the gastrointestinal tract such as bile. Vomiting may be due to the type of substance ingested (for example, rotten food) or the brain reaction due to tumors, pregnancy, nervous stress, and so on. The condition that occurs in a patient before vomiting is called nausea (feeling of uplift).
