Coexistence

In Dehkhoda dictionary, coexistence means living together. Two people or two groups living together while they may not be suitable for each other. . . . In the forest, the boys are naked among the piles of plants, beside the ponds, indifferent and busy. Large, gigantic, yet calm animals. Boys and animals sometimes ignore each other, sometimes next to each other and sometimes in each other. It is strange that they are not as enthusiastic as I am! Do not feel the danger? Is their current situation incomprehensible to them? I have no answers to these questions! Maybe something else will happen at night that will disturb the playful and calm boys from this closeness, make them regret this coexistence ...
